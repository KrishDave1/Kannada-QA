Ramesh Balutagi, a resident of Kushtagi from Kopal, has made significant strides in the timber industry. A devout Christian, he is known for his work titled "Gandhada Naadanna" (The Melody of Gandhi) and has established himself as a prominent figure with the success of his venture called Sririvantanagar after acquiring the title 'Sririvanda.' Under his guidance, over 250 young individuals are employed at the Sririvandha Parimala Hub situated on a vast expanse of 1500 acres in Kushtagi. This hub not only epitomizes Ramesh's achievements but also serves as a model for rural youth on how to build sustainable livelihoods through wood-based agriculture initiatives. His endeavors have earned recognition by Sivarna News, highlighting both his personal accomplishments and contributions to the local community.
